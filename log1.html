Understanding Log Book

Whether due to lax credit standards, poor portfolio risk management, or weakness in the economy, loan portfolio problems have historically been the major cause of business losses and failures. Systematic recording of daily or hourly activities, events, and/or occurrences is done in a log book.Here we have focused on the customers' payment activities. Keep a check on the amount that the customers owe you with the Log Book feature.

Managing the Log Book

Keeping track of credit due is made much easier with the log book management feature of the website. Both the View and the Update options in the website increase the efficiency of financial management. Securing this page is essential to make sure that only the admin is able to access the page.

Generating the Bill

Saamagri also allows you to integrate bill generation with the log book. So, the need for manual maintenance of the customer credits is eliminated. The bill also automatically takes care of the outstanding credit due of a customer. It helps the business owner to keep track of the daily sales from his store.
